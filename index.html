About HWEC2025

The Healthcare Workforce Equity Conference 2025 brings together healthcare professionals, students, educators, policy makers, and organizations involved in workforce development.

Our mission is to address systemic workforce challenges, promote inclusive and supportive environments, and create fair opportunities in healthcare for all.

Conference Strands

Join us for six dynamic strands designed to address key aspects of healthcare workforce equity. Each strand offers unique insights and opportunities for collaboration.

General Sessions

Start your conference experience with inspiring keynotes and welcome sessions that set the stage for collaborative learning and networking. Gain valuable insights from thought leaders shaping the future of healthcare workforce equity.

  • Inspiring keynote addresses
  • Thought leadership presentations
  • Interactive panel discussions

Diversifying the Healthcare Workforce

Explore comprehensive strategies for building an inclusive healthcare workforce that reflects the diversity of our communities.

  • Diversity competencies
  • Workplace dynamics
  • Innovative recruitment strategies
  • Retention best practices

Post Secondary and Higher Education

Discover how educational institutions are creating equitable pathways to healthcare careers through innovation and industry collaboration.

  • Educational pathway development
  • Industry-academic partnerships
  • Training program innovation
  • Career advancement opportunities

Equity-Centered Public Health Policies

Engage in shaping policies that prioritize equity and efficiency in healthcare workforce development.

  • Policy development strategies
  • Stakeholder engagement
  • Multicultural workforce initiatives
  • Oregon-specific solutions

Trade Show Networking

Connect with industry leaders and discover innovative solutions in healthcare workforce development.

  • Interactive exhibits
  • Resource discovery
  • Professional networking
  • Industry innovations

Traditional Health Workers Alliance Hub

Learn about the vital role of Traditional Health Workers and explore sustainable models for workforce development.

  • THW impact assessment
  • Funding strategies
  • Operational best practices
  • Workforce resilience

Each strand offers unique opportunities for learning, networking, and collaboration. Choose your focus areas and join us in shaping the future of healthcare workforce equity.
